goth pumpkin chocolate chip muffins

ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup canola/vegetable oil
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 tsps baking powder
  • 2 tsps baking soda
  • 2 tssps cinnamon
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 can pure pumpkin
  • chocolate chips

steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. mix all of the dry ingreients
  3. add wet ingredients and mix
  4. stir in desired amount of chocolate chips
  5. bake for 25 mins

backgroud

This recipe comes from my childhood best friend's mom; she would make these all the time when I went over their house in elementary school. As I got older she shared the recipe with my mom and I and we'd make them all the time.
